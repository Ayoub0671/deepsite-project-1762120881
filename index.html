Our Story

A Fusion of Two Culinary Worlds

Founded in 2010 by Chef Hiroshi Tanaka and Chef Supatra Wong, Sakura & Lotus brings together the delicate artistry of Japanese cuisine with the bold, vibrant flavors of Thailand. Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a dining experience that honors both traditions while innovating for the modern palate.

Every dish at Sakura & Lotus is crafted with the finest ingredients, sourced sustainably and prepared with meticulous attention to detail. Our sushi rice is seasoned to perfection, our Thai herbs are freshly imported, and every cut, every spice, every presentation is executed with care.

We invite you to experience this unique culinary harmony in our intimate, zen-inspired space where every meal is a celebration of flavor, texture, and cultural connection.
